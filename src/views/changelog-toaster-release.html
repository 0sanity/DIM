v3.17.0 - Our Million User Update

Just wanted to say thanks to everyone who has used DIM in the past two years. We're thankful for having a part of your Destiny 1 experience, and we're looking forward to the future.

  • Updated maxiumum artifact bonus to 43.
  • Add tags and notes to items! This has been in Beta forever but now it's official. Hit ? to see the keyboard shortcuts, and use "tag:" searches to find your tagged gear. ** Tags and notes are not synced.
  • Vendors now show where they are, and are sorted better. All the cryptarchs now appear. Engrams waiting to be decrypted aren't shown in the vendor screen.
  • Style updates to clean up DIM's look and make sure more screen space is being used for items.
  • The "Hide Unfiltered Items while Filtering" preference now applies to vendor gear too.
  • When moving stacks of items through the popup, there are now buttons to max out the amount, and add and remove up to even stacks of items.
  • And more...
